Our Story

We Built This
For Our Own Boutique

We were also in the rental business. During wedding season, it was completely hectic — flipping through receipt books, checking handwritten calendars to see if a dress was already booked, doing profit calculations by hand every night.

Returns were not tracked properly. Revenue was just a guess. And when a customer called asking about their booking, we had to search through pages of register entries.

Today, everything is on WhatsApp and mobile — but our boutique management was still stuck in 2005. So we built BoutiqueOS to solve our own problem first.
